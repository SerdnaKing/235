BRIEF HOW 2 PLAY: Click on the circles as they appear to gain points. You have one minute to click as many as you can before the game's over! On the bottom-left you'll see three clocks. Each resets the countdown timer to one minute, also resetting all the circles to be motionless once again for a limited time! Lastly, the Yellow Targets move faster over time, while the Purple Targets don't, but burst into 2 of the Yellow when clicked!

The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past

Publishing Information

  • Published by: Nintendo Co., Ltd.
  • Developed by: Nintendo EAD
  • Released: Nov 21, 1991
  • Platforms: SNES, Wii, Wii U, New Nintendo 3DS, Nintendo Switch

Description

The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past is a top-down single-player action game with puzzle-solving elements (similar to the original Legend of Zelda)
Players assume the role of Link, and their goal is to rescue Princess Zelda and save the land of Hyrule. [...]

Game Categories

  • Genre: Action
  • Perspective: Bird's-eye view
  • Art: Anime / manga
  • Gameplay: Metroidvania, Puzzle elements
  • Interface: Direct control
  • Setting: Fantasy
